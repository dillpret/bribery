Quick Start

Bribery is a party game about creativity and persuasion:

  1. Each player creates a prompt for others to respond to
  2. You'll create bribes to win points from other players
  3. You'll vote for your favourite bribe from others
  4. Player with most points wins!

How a Round Works

  1. Prompt Creation: Choose a prompt for others to use when bribing you
  2. Bribe Creation: Craft creative bribes for your assigned targets
  3. Voting: Review the bribes you received and vote for your favourite
  4. Results: See who won points and current standings

Tips for Better Bribes

  • Be creative - You can use text, images, GIFs, or links
  • Know your audience - Personalize bribes to your target
  • Keep it fun - The goal is entertainment, not serious competition

Game Settings

The host can adjust:

  • Number of rounds (3, 5, or 7)
  • Time limits for each phase
  • Custom prompt options
