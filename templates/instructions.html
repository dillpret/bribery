How Bribery Works

Bribery is a creative game of persuasion and voting:

  1. Each round, you'll receive a point to award to one of your bribers
  2. You'll choose a prompt that other players will use when bribing you
  3. Two other players will submit creative "bribes" to win your point
  4. You'll vote for your favourite bribe and award them your point
  5. Meanwhile, you'll be creating bribes to win points from other players
  6. The player with the most points at the end wins!

Detailed Gameplay

Round Structure:

  1. Target Selection: Each player selects a prompt for others to use when bribing them
  2. Bribe Creation: Players create bribes for their assigned targets
  3. Voting: Players vote on the bribes they received
  4. Results: Points are awarded and scores are updated

Bribes Can Include:

  • Text responses
  • Images and GIFs
  • Links to videos or websites
  • Any creative content that might win a vote!

Tips for Success

  • Be creative with prompts: Choose prompts that inspire fun and creative responses
  • Know your audience: Personalize your bribes to what you know about the other player
  • Have fun with it: Don't be afraid to be silly or over-the-top
  • Mix it up: Try different types of bribes (text, images, etc.) to see what works best
  • Keep it appropriate: Remember that bribes are visible to everyone in the game

Game Settings

The host can customize:

  • Number of rounds: Choose how long the game will last
  • Time limits: Set optional timers for each phase
  • Custom prompts: Use community-created prompts or defaults
